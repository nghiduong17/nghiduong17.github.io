Project:
3D Face Modeling

Reconstruction a 3D face model from single 2D image. Current state of my work is fitting 3D Morphable model to 2D image using hard edge correspondence and landmark correspondence (Done- Link to code). Next steps are to make the model work with low resolution image, estimate texture and expression.



Face In The Wild Alignment

This is pre-processing step of large scale face images (data from megaFace project of University of Washington). This pre-process does landmark detection, align eye level, crop face image and scale to certain size. (link to code)
